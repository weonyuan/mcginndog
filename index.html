About McGinnDog

Inspired by the McGinnDog, prepare for a game that will have you thinking and engaged with some fine tunes handpicked by the man himself. The app uses Spotify Web API in order to retrieve the songs from Spotify's servers, and thus requires a registered Spotify account.

Playlist

The playlist is comprised of McGinnDog's favorite songs - meaning songs from Eminem and Fall Out Boy.

Dependencies

McGinnDog depends on AngularJS, Bootstrap, and angular-spotify (to communicate with Spotify Web API).
